Bio

Daniel I. Copeland, MD, MSc is a physician-engineer focused on psychiatry research and translational mind-body medicine. His work integrates clinical neuroscience with quantitative sensing methods to improve measurement and treatment in depression and related conditions. He collaborates across medicine, engineering, and data science to build practical tools for real-world clinical care.

Academic Background

  • MD training with emphasis in psychiatry.
  • MSc-level quantitative and translational research training.
  • Ongoing interdisciplinary collaboration in clinical and computational domains.
